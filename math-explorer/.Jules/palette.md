## 2024-05-22 - [Autism-Friendly Mission Theming]
**Learning:** For children aged 7-10 on the autism spectrum, replacing a generic "Math Quiz" with a "Mission Map" narrative significantly reduces performance anxiety by framing challenges as exploration rather than testing.
**Action:** Use "Mission Station" and "Pilot" terminology in future educational apps for this demographic to improve engagement and emotional regulation.

## 2024-05-22 - [Keyboard Shortcuts for Cognitive Ease]
**Learning:** Adding numeric keyboard shortcuts (1-4) for multiple-choice answers, accompanied by visible kbd badges, helps users who find drag-and-drop or precise mouse movement overstimulating or difficult.
**Action:** Always provide keyboard-first alternatives for core interactions in accessibility-focused UI.
